How We Build Intelligence

From Raw Signals to Decision-Grade Data

We do not provide raw data. We structure fragmented industrial signals into actionable, highly defensible intelligence built for enterprise decision-makers.

1. Defensible Sources

All datasets are built on a rigorous combination of vectors:

  • Institutional: Financial reports, official PR, public tenders (e.g., Consip).
  • Technological: Vendor documentation (AWS, Microsoft), engineering job postings.
  • Market: Industrial news, strategic partnerships, MoUs.
  • Signal Intelligence: Targeted hiring, tech stack migrations, compliance certifications.

2. The Intelligence Pipeline

  1. Collection: Gathering raw data across the aforementioned sources.
  2. Normalization: Standardizing Cloud, ERP, CI/CD, and Observability assets.
  3. Scoring: Assigning proprietary metrics (e.g., Lock-in Score, Migration Score).
  4. Correlation Layer: Connecting the dots (e.g., Hiring + Cloud + ERP = Active Transformation).
  5. Actionable Output: Categorizing signals into ENGAGE, MONITOR, or STRATEGIC.
