How We Build Intelligence

From Raw Signals to Decision-Grade Data

We do not provide raw data feeds. We structure fragmented industrial signals into actionable, highly defensible intelligence built for strategic planning.

1. The Intelligence Pipeline

  1. Collect: Gathering technical, institutional, and market signals.
  2. Normalize: Standardizing Cloud, ERP, and Edge assets into a unified format.
  3. Score: Applying metrics like Lock-in Score and Migration Score.
  4. Correlate: Connecting the dots (e.g., Hiring + Tech Spikes = Transformation).
  5. Deliver: Categorizing signals into ENGAGE, MONITOR, or STRATEGIC.

2. Trust & Data Principles

  • Public, licensed and proprietary signals where applicable.
  • Structured for strategic intelligence and risk analysis.
  • Handled under applicable permissions and data practices.
